  • Drink 3–4 liters of water per day — more on training days.

  • Maintain a 300–500 kcal deficit — anything larger risks muscle loss.

  • Cut carbs on rest days; keep them around your training sessions.
  • Do not skip post-workout nutrition — it's critical for muscle preservation.
  • Prioritize sleep: poor sleep elevates cortisol and increases fat storage.

Key Supplements

Food first — these fill gaps, not replace meals.

Whey Protein
Fast-absorbing post-workout protein to hit daily targets easily.

Creatine Monohydrate
Most research-backed supplement for strength and lean mass gains.
3–5g daily

Critical for testosterone production, bone density, and immunity.
